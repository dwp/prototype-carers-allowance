You cannot get Carer's Allowance if you are under 16

You entered your date of birth as 9 September 2010, meaning you are under 16.

You can only apply for Carer's Allowance up to 3 months before you turn 16. Based on the date of birth you entered, this means you can apply again from 9 June 2026.
