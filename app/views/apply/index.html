Breaks in care - iteration 6

{# Experimental #}
Date of design
February 2022
Designers
Emily W and Colin J

Business requirements

  • If citizen provides dates of a break in care that run over a part week, we need to know whether or not they cared for 35 hours in that week

Research scenario

User is making a claim for CA. They started providing care for John Smith on 12 November 2021, and they’re claiming CA from 5 January 2022.

John went into hospital on 11 January 2022 and came out on 26 January 2022. The user identifies this as being a break in care and needs to tell us. The care weeks in which she did not provide 35 hours of care are 16 to 22 January and 23 to 29 January. She provided care for 35 hours in the care week where John went into hospital (9 to 15 January).

Related story

  • ACA-1386 - Breaks in care - iteration 2 - citizen can confirm part weeks as a break

Design 1

The user is presented with both of the part-care weeks on one page and asked to tell us which ones they did not provide 35 hours of care, if any.

Start now
Design 2

The user is asked about each part-care week as separate questions.

PRO517 automation improvements - iteration 1

{# Experimental #}
Date of design
December 2021
Designers
Colin J

Business requirements

In order to improve the current PRO517 automation rate (66%), we need to increase the number of claims that come in with the DP's NINO. Currently, around 60% of cases where the PRO517 failed to sent automatically were because there was no DP NINO.

Looking at ways to change the way we capture the DP NINO, encouraging users to enter it where they can.

We are unable to make this field mandatory.

Related story

  • ACA-1311 - Improvement of automation of PRO517 letters
